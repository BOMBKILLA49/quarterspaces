New Mexico Tiny Homes

Explore our collection of New Mexico tiny homes, designed for sustainable and affordable living in the Land of Enchantment. Quarterspaces crafts compact, eco-friendly homes tailored to your lifestyle, blending modern design with the rugged beauty of New Mexico.

Why Choose New Mexico Tiny Homes?

New Mexico tiny homes by Quarterspaces offer a unique blend of affordability, sustainability, and customization. Whether you're seeking an off-grid retreat in the deserts of Taos or a cozy home near the vibrant culture of Santa Fe, our tiny homes are built to meet your needs. Each home is crafted with eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient designs, and accessibility features, ensuring comfort without compromising on style.

  • Energy-efficient designs with solar-ready options
  • Customizable layouts to fit your lifestyle
  • FSC-certified lumber and sustainable materials
  • Compact sizes ranging from 12x30 to 16x30
  • Built to withstand New Mexico’s diverse climate

Features of Our Tiny Homes

Our New Mexico tiny homes are designed with quality and functionality in mind. From durable 2x6 framing to weatherproof Zip System wall panels, every detail is crafted for longevity. Enjoy modern amenities like fiberglass windows, composite doors, and gambrel roofing, all tailored to blend with New Mexico’s stunning landscapes.

  1. 2x6 framing with 4-inch thick insulated walls
  2. 26-gauge Galvalume corrugated metal roofing
  3. Complete plumbing and electrical systems
  4. Tight seal under 0.28 CFM75/ft2 for energy efficiency

Our homes are perfect for those looking to downsize, live sustainably, or create a vacation retreat in New Mexico’s breathtaking scenery.
